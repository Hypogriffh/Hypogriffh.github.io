My Gamedev Journey

I have been developing video games since I was 14 years old. I started out with mostly private projects before releasing my first game, a 2D sandbox caving game made in Unity called Fissure. Since then, I've been practicing non-stop to improve my skills and use my tools more efficiently. I am primarily a 2D game developer, but I have dabbled and gotten familiar with 3D games in private projects.

I currently specialize in the following frameworks/engines:

  • Unity
  • FNA/MonoGame
  • Godot Engine
  • RPG Maker
  • GameMaker Studio
  • Unreal Engine 4

My Education

I am currently in the process of pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. Apart from that, I am largely self-taught for the better part of 5 years. I find the best way to learn something is to do something.

Why I Make Games

As a kid, I was always fascinated by video games. Not only did I want to play them, I wanted to dissect them and discover how they worked. This motivation to deeply understand my favorite games naturally evolved into a desire to make video games myself. Like many others, I consider video games to be an art form, and I have a deep desire to express myself through interactive media. It's also incredibly fun!
